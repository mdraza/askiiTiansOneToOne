FAQ's

What's the duration of each class?

The student enrolls as by selecting the hours of one-one sessions he/she needs. The duration of the session conducted gets deducted from the total hours. The duration completely depends on the student and the subject the student wants to learn. On average, a session will have 1.5 hours.

How many classes are conducted in a week?

Weekly 6 days are available to conduct your classes. You can frame your time table from MONDAY TO SATURDAY. Sunday is not a working day.

What's the experience level of teachers?

Askiitians has 100+ faculties with an average experience of 5+ years and from premier institutes like IITs, AIIMS, IISC, and other top universities. See Our Faculty Team

Can I choose my own time to study /learn?

Yes. The student can choose his/her own time but it has to be mentioned prior to the match the availability of the teacher.

Do you conduct a timely test?

If the student wants a test can be designed and be taken by the teacher. So, yes there are timely tests.
